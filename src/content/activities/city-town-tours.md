  Explore the rich history, culture, and architecture of Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage site, or experience the local hustle of Arusha town. Wander through narrow streets, historic palaces, and bustling spice markets with a knowledgeable Swahili guide.
  
  In Stone Town, trace the steps of Sultans, Swahili merchants, and explorers. Marvel at the famous carved wooden doors, visit the Old Slave Market, the House of Wonders, and the Palace Museum. Smell the fresh cardamom, cloves, and vanilla at a local spice farm outside the town, or shop for vibrant kanga fabrics in the bustling local bazaars.
