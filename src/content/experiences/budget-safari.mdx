  A budget camping safari is the most affordable way to see Tanzania's wildlife without sacrificing the quality of the guiding or the routes. The toilets and showers are shared and simple, but the experience is real — the sounds of the bush at night, the stars above the campfire, the proximity to the animals.

  Our camping safaris are private, with a 4x4 pop-top vehicle, a senior guide, a chef, and the full crew. They are designed for travellers who want the safari experience without the lodge price tag — typically $2,500 to $3,800 per person sharing for a 6-7 day northern circuit.
