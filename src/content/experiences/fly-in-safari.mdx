    a: Scheduled flight times between Zanzibar and Serengeti airstrips (like Seronera) typically take between 1.5 to 2.5 hours, depending on any intermediate stops in Arusha or Lake Manyara.
  - q: What is the luggage allowance on fly-in safaris?
    a: Since fly-in safaris use light aircraft (like Cessna Caravans), there is a strict luggage limit of 15kg (33 lbs) per person, which must be packed in soft-sided bags. Hard suitcases cannot fit in the baggage pods.
