Mount Meru is the secret weapon of experienced Africa travelers. The climb is challenging in its own right — the knife-edge ridge is genuinely thrilling — but it's also the perfect way to acclimatize before Kilimanjaro. Many of our guests climb Meru, descend, rest for two days, then tackle Kilimanjaro. They summit at a much higher rate than those who go straight to Kibo. If you have the time, this is the move.
