The Mara River crossing is the image that comes to mind when anyone says 'Great Migration.' A million wildebeest in the water, Nile crocodiles lunging, the desperate scramble up the far bank. It's chaotic, violent, and one of the most extraordinary wildlife events on Earth. To see it, you need to be in the right place at the right time, and our mobile camps have been tracking the action for over a decade.
