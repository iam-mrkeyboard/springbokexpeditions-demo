Machame is the route that launched a thousand Kilimanjaro dreams. Since the first trekker walked this path in the 1970s, it has remained the favorite of climbers who want scenery, challenge, and a genuine chance of summiting. The seven-day itinerary is the sweet spot — long enough to acclimatize properly, short enough to fit into a two-week Tanzania trip. If you have the time for one climb in your life, this is the one.
