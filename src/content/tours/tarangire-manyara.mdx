This is the safari we recommend for anyone who has just three or four days in Tanzania, or for Kilimanjaro climbers who want to add a safari before or after their climb. Tarangire and Manyara are close to Arusha, the wildlife is fantastic, and the price is right. Perfect for a long-weekend stopover, or as a build-your-own add-on.
