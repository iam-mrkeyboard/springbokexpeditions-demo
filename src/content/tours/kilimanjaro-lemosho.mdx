Lemosho is the route we recommend to anyone who has the time. The eight-day itinerary gives your body the best possible chance to acclimatize, the western start is uncrowded and beautiful, and the southern circuit approach means the final days have some of the best summit-day views in Africa. It's a longer commitment than Machame, but the trade-off is a 96% summit rate and a climb you'll remember for the rest of your life.
