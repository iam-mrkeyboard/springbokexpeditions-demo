## Day Trip Options

**Ngorongoro Crater Day Trip** (US$450 pp) — The classic day trip. Descend into the crater, see the Big Five, lunch by the hippo pool, ascend and return to Arusha. Early start (6am), back by 6pm.

**Tarangire Day Trip** (US$350 pp) — Full-day game drive in Tarangire with picnic lunch. Elephants, baobabs, and the Tarangire River. Back to Arusha by 5pm.

**Lake Manyara Day Trip** (US$320 pp) — Morning and afternoon game drives in Manyara, with lunch at a lakeside lodge. Tree-climbing lions, flamingos, and Rift Valley views. Back to Arusha by 5pm.

**Arusha National Park Day Trip** (US$280 pp) — Close to the city, but feels a world away. Canoeing on Momella Lake (optional), walking safaris with an armed ranger, and a chance to see black-and-white colobus monkeys. Back to Arusha by 4pm.

**Materuni Waterfalls & Coffee Tour** (US$220 pp) — Hike to the 80m Materuni Falls in the foothills of Kilimanjaro, then a traditional coffee tour with a Chagga family. Swim in the natural pool at the base of the falls. Back to Moshi by 4pm.

**Marangu Cultural Day Tour** (US$180 pp) — Visit a Chagga village, learn about traditional banana beer brewing, see a local market, and hike through the lush lower slopes of Kilimanjaro. Includes a traditional Chagga lunch. Back to Moshi by 4pm.

Book any combination — or ask us to design a custom day trip for your group.
