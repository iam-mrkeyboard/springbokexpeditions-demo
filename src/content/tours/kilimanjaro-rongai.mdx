Rongai is the route we recommend for those who want to climb Kilimanjaro without the crowds. The northern approach sees a fraction of the climbers of the southern routes, and the gentler gradients make the trekking itself more enjoyable. The trade-off is less dramatic scenery, but for many climbers, the solitude is worth it. Pair it with a safari for a complete Tanzania adventure.
