The Northern Circuit is the safari that started it all for most of our guests, and for good reason: in seven days, you can encounter the Big Five, watch a million wildebeest thunder across the plains, walk among Maasai warriors, and descend into a volcanic caldera that shelters more wildlife than almost anywhere else on Earth.

What makes this itinerary special isn't just the parks — it's the pacing. We've designed it with full days rather than rushed drives, with stays inside the parks so you can experience the bush at dawn and dusk, and with thoughtful touches (a Maasai village visit, a picnic by the hippo pool, sundowners overlooking the crater) that turn a safari into a story you'll tell for the rest of your life.
