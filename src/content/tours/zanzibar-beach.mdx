We could just send you to a beach resort and call it done. We do not. The Stone Town tour is led by a local guide who grew up in the narrow alleys, who can tell you why the doors are the way they are, where the best street food is, and what life was like during the sultans. The spice farm is not a tourist trap - it is a working farm, and the family who runs it has been growing cloves and vanilla for four generations. The dhow cruise is a chance to slow down, the way Zanzibar always used to.
