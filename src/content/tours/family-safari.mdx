We've designed this for kids aged 5 to 15. The drives are shorter, the activities are hands-on, and the lodges are chosen for their family rooms and pools. Our guides are parents themselves, and they know how to keep children engaged — telling stories, playing spotting games, and making the safari feel like an adventure rather than a lecture. We've had 7-year-olds identify 30+ animals by the end of the trip.
