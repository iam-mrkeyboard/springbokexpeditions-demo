Four days is tight, but it works. The Ngorongoro Crater packs so much wildlife into one place that you can see the Big Five in a single morning. Tarangire complements it perfectly — different scenery, different animals, and the chance to spend a relaxed full day in the bush. If you can add a few days, extend to the Serengeti for the full Northern Circuit experience.
