Marangu has a reputation as the 'easy' route, but no Kilimanjaro climb is easy. What Marangu offers is hut accommodation — real beds in a wooden A-frame — which makes the experience feel a little less rugged. The 6-day itinerary adds an acclimatization day at Horombo, which is critical for summit success. If you want a comfortable, well-supported climb with all the logistics handled, this is the route for you.
