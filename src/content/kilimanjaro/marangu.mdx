The Marangu Route is the only path on Kilimanjaro that uses built hut accommodation, which makes it a strong choice for travellers who prefer a bed to a tent and want hot meals served in a dining room. The trail is the most gradual of all the routes, and the path is the widest and best-maintained on the mountain.

The trade-off is traffic - Marangu is the busiest route, especially during the dry season. If solitude matters, consider Machame, Lemosho or the Northern Circuit instead.
