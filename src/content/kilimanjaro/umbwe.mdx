Umbwe is not the route for a first-time Kilimanjaro climber. The two days of 1,000m+ elevation gains leave almost no room for acclimatization, and the summit success rate reflects that. But for fit, experienced mountaineers looking for a real challenge in relative solitude, Umbwe is the most rewarding route on the mountain.
