Rongai is the right route for climbers who want a quieter, drier path - particularly those climbing outside the main June–October and January–February windows. The northern approach is less dramatic than Lemosho, but the trail is gentle, the camps are excellent, and the sense of solitude is hard to find elsewhere on the mountain.
