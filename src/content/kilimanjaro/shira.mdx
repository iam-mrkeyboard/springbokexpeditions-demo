The Shira Route is the right choice for climbers who are confident at altitude and want a quieter version of the Lemosho experience. The 4×4 transfer up the western slopes is a memorable start, and the Shira Plateau is one of the most spectacular landscapes on the mountain. The trade-off is less natural acclimatization on the first day, so we recommend this route to climbers with previous high-altitude experience.
