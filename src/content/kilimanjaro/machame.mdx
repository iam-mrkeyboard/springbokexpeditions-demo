The Machame Route is the route we recommend most often. The seven-day version is long enough for proper acclimatization, short enough to be a realistic commitment, and scenic enough to feel like a real adventure from start to finish. The descent via Mweka means you see different scenery on the way down, which keeps the trip interesting all week.
